# 🏛️ Minerva MOC (Map of Content)

This is the central routing hub for the knowledge base. 

## 📝 Manager Review & Alignments
*Active statements of work and cross-functional task proposals.*
- **[[for_review/]]** — *Folder for all `type: alignment` and `type: pitch` memos.*
- **Workflow:** Draft (`pending_review`) -> Manager Approval -> Update to `approved` -> Agent routes to Hephaestus.

## 🧬 Knowledge Graph (The Wiki)
*The agent-maintained Open Knowledge Format (OKF) repository.*
- **[[wiki/concepts/|Concepts & Assays]]** — Biological entities, trial phases, and computational pipelines.
- **[[wiki/claims/|Clinical Claims]]** — Synthesized hypotheses and contradictory trial literature.
- **[[wiki/datasets/|Datasets & Pipelines]]** — Clinical data batches, QC states, and Snakemake commit hashes.
- **[[wiki/entities/|Stakeholder Directory]]** — Internal clinical leads, bioinformaticians, and external vendor profiles.
- **[[wiki/papers/|Literature Node]]** — Extracted clinical trial papers and multidimensional assay findings.
- **[[wiki/methodologies/|Methodologies]]** — Computational tools, R/Python packages (e.g., brms, scVI), and analytical pipelines.

- **[[daily_notes/|Daily Notes]]** — Minor milestones and chronological meeting aggregations.
- **[[raw_sources/|Raw Event Stubs]]** — Pre-generated calendar meeting stubs awaiting manual note entry.
